Land

With a long experience in the transport of bulky manufactured goods and industrial equipment in North Africa, supported by our network made up of European partners specialized in exceptional transport, we are able to provide our customers with the right solution for all "large" transport.
A careful study even before the start of transport is the key to success in connecting Europe and North Africa by transporting materials of exceptional dimensions with heavy weights on special vehicles. Low-bed semi-trailers, trays and technical escorts are available after having analyzed in detail the packing list and the best route to reach the target.
The choice of shipping company and boarding methods are studied beforehand and carefully, so that on both sides of the Mediterranean, exceptional transport goods can travel safely within the framework of local regulations.


MULTIMODAL TRANSPORTATION REEFER

Reefer advanced technology: for the management of the cold chain system, a GPS / WEB with advanced technology.
For the transport of goods at controlled temperature, we adopt a system of advanced technology GPS / WEB which detects the positions of the vehicles and the operating data of each refrigerated trailer, by constantly recording the temperatures compared to the programmed set point and how the system works, reporting any critical situation in real time.
This methodology allows operators to intervene quickly to resolve any problems.
Thus the good preservation of the goods is ensured and monitored.

REGULATIONS AND HACCP STANDARDS ARE THE INDISCUTABLE REFERENCE POINTS FOR ALL OUR ORGANIZATION OF THE TRANSPORT OF THE AGRI-FOOD SECTOR, FOOD & BEVERAGE, PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTS AND HORTICULTURE.
